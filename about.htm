How to make tea:

  1. Pick your desired loose tea.
  2. Put your desired amount into a tea strainer.
  3. Boil desired amount of water.
  4. Put strainer filled with the loose tea into the boiling water.
  5. Let the strainer sit in the water for about 5 to 10 minutes.
  6. Remove strainer and enjoy!
  • The amount of loose tea and water depend on how strong or weak you want the tea to be.
